Welcome to js Whack A Mole!

To play, simply click on each mole to get rid of it!

The goal is to be able to get rid of all 12 moles in the allotted 20 seconds.

The moles change position every 3 seconds and it's important to note than an additional mole will be added each time the moles change position.
